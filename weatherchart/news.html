Editorial rules

A link is better than a borrowed article

WeatherChart retains a source title and publication date, writes a short original take, and links directly to the official page. It does not scrape article HTML, reproduce article bodies, hotlink Met Office images, or insert a redirect.

If an automated feed item has not been manually checked, the safe wording is neutral: “A new Met Office update is available—read the official source for full details.”
